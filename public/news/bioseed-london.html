Best Presenter at BioUK BioSeed 2026, London

June 2026 · AdCab

AdCab's Scientific Lead, Dr. Firas Hamdan, was named best presenter in his session at BioUK's BioSeed 2026 event in London. The event brought together 40 early-stage life sciences companies pitching to more than 80 active investors and funders, with founders connecting through presentations, networking, and partnering meetings.

BioSeed is one of the UK's leading platforms for early-stage biotech companies to access investor feedback and forge new partnerships. AdCab's recognition there reflects the strength of the science and the team's ability to communicate a compelling vision for reaching the clinic.
