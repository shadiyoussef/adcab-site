Best Pitch at the Finnish Biotech & Investor Summit

June 15, 2026 · Dr Firas Hamdan

Yesterday, I had the pleasure of taking part in my first pitching competition at the Finnish Biotech and Investor Summit.

I am extremely grateful to the audience for voting me as the best pitch. It was a truly special moment, especially because I was pitching alongside ten other excellent biotech companies, each presenting inspiring ideas and strong visions for the future of healthcare. I learned a lot from every pitch and from the discussions throughout the day.

A big thank you to Tesi, Innovestor and the Finnish Drug Discovery Center Ltd., Suomen Lääkekehityskeskus Oy for organizing such an excellent event and for creating a platform that brings together biotech innovators, investors, and key stakeholders in the Finnish life science ecosystem.

This was an important milestone for me and for our work, and I am very grateful for the encouragement and support.
